OpenContext Research Capture Policy (editable)

Purpose
- Decide whether recent research activity should require an OpenContext checkpoint.
- This text is passed to the research-capture classifier model.

Classification
- require_capture=true for implementation-relevant external research:
  docs/specs/papers/GitHub/similar-project findings that influenced decisions, fixes, architecture, or debugging strategy.
- require_capture=false for routine local exploration with no meaningful external insight to preserve.
- If activity appears similar to previously solved hurdles in GCC history, prefer require_capture=true.

Output contract (strict JSON):
- require_capture: boolean
- reason: string
- confidence: number (0..1)
